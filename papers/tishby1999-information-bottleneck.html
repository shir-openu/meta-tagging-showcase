findingRelevant information in X about Y is extracted by squeezing X through a bottleneck of codewords that preserves maximum information about Y — a variational principle generalizing rate-distortion theory, with distortion emerging from the joint statistics of X and Y. — “we squeeze the information that X provides about Y through a ‘bottleneck’ formed by a limited set of codewords ̃X. This constrained optimization problem can be seen as a generalization of rate distortion theory in which the distortion measure d(x, ̃x) emerges from the joint statistics of X and Y .”
findingThe method yields an exact set of self-consistent equations solvable by a convergent re-estimation generalizing the Blahut–Arimoto algorithm. — “This approach yields an exact set of self consistent equations for the coding rules X → ̃X and ̃X → Y . Solutions to these equations can be found by a convergent re–estimation method that generalizes the Blahut–Arimoto algorithm.”
