The finding — statistics succeed as join keys where free text failed. The preprint's central null result was that the free-written phenomenon facet, worded too specifically, shared a string across only 1 pair of disciplines. Canonical statistical terms are the opposite: because a p-value, a standard deviation or a hypothesis test means the same thing in physics, biology, psychology and sociology, each bridges many fields at once — mean spans 33 disciplines, statistical-significance 27, standard-deviation 25, correlation 24, p-value 19. This is the field-invariant join key the phenomenon facet was missing.
