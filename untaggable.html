Characteristic signal so far: untaggable papers skew older (median year 2007.0 vs 2013.5 for tagged) and paywalled (50% personal-copy vs 9% among tagged) — i.e. this correlates with age & access, not reliability. The reliability signal lives elsewhere: in the facet profile of papers that tag successfully (bold claim + no stated limitations + untested/failed replication) — a separate scan.
